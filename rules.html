Da Rules

Goal of the Game
The main goal is to checkmate your opponent’s king by putting them in a position where it is under attack and cannot escape.

The Board
The chessboard has 8 rows (1–8) and 8 columns (a–h), forming 64 squares in total.
Each player starts with 16 pieces:
- 1 King
- 1 Queen
- 2 Rooks
- 2 Bishops
- 2 Knights
- 8 Pawns

Starting the Game
White always moves first, then players take turns one at a time.

Basic Movement
- Pawns move forward but capture diagonally.
- Rooks move in straight lines.
- Bishops move diagonally.
- Knights move in an “L” shape and can jump over pieces.
- The Queen moves in any direction.
- The King moves one square in any direction.

Winning the Game
You win by putting the opponent’s king in checkmate — meaning it’s under threat and has no legal move to escape.
