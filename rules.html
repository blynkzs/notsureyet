Goal of the Game The main goal is to checkmate your opponent’s king by putting them in a position to be captured with no way to escape. The Board The chessboard has 8 rows (1–8) and 8 columns (a–h). Each player starts with 16 pieces: 1 King 1 Queen 2 Rooks 2 Bishops 2 Knights 8 Pawns White moves first, and then players take turns.
